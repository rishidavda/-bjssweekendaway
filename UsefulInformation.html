A few things to enjoy your weekend..

Check-in & check-out

Check-in on Saturday is at 3 pm – bags can be left at reception and collected later.

Check-out on Sunday is at 11 am – bags can be left at reception, leisure facilities will be available until departure.

Wristbands

A wristband will be issued to each guest on arrival. It will also be your free drinks pass at the bar, so please keep it on during your stay.

If you have children, please add your contact details to their wristbands.

Leisure facilities

Leisure facilities are free of charge and open in the hours between 6.30 am - 10 pm. Latest entry is 9:30 pm. Under 18s are not allowed in the pool after 8 pm.

Under 16s cannot be in the pool without an adult. One adult can supervise up to two children under 16s.

Adult only swim times: 12 pm - 2 pm and 7 pm - 10 pm.

Gym – if you want to use the gym, please ask reception for the code

Pool, sauna and steam room – you can use them until depature

Showers – you can use them until depature

Spa treatments – extra charges apply for spa treatments and will need to be settled directly with the hotel at guests' own expense

Beverages

The BJSS inclusive bar will be limited to a selection of wines, beers, spirits & soft drinks. Any other drinks will need to be settled directly with the hotel at guests' own expense.
