Leisure Facilities
• Leisure facilities are free of charge and open between 6.30-22.00. Latest entry 21.30.
• Under 18s are not allowed in the pool after 8.00pm. Under 16s cannot be in the pool without an adult. One adult can supervise up to two under 16s.
• Adult only swim times 12.00-14.00 and 19.00-22.00
