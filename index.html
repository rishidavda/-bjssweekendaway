Welcome to Nottingham Strategy Weekend 2019

Carden Park Hotel
20th - 21st July


I am delighted to welcome you to the beautiful Carden Park Hotel. The Strategy Weekend is always a special date in our calendar, which gives us the perfect opportunity to bring everyone together and to thank you and your families for the awesome work and dedication you have shown to BJSS.

This year’s particularly special in that BJSS Nottingham has now reached a size where we need a strategy weekend of our own! We all know the Nottingham team needs little encouragement to have a great time - so I look forward to seeing you, your partners and families enjoying the activities we’ve organised and facilities available.

BJSS simply cannot exist without you. The quality of the work, and the spirit and pride with which you deliver it is invaluable. It is what makes BJSS - especially Nottingham - what it is and it’s the reason our clients continue to work with us to help their businesses thrive in this digital age.

Throughout the weekend you will have the opportunity to kick back and relax with colleagues and friends and get involved with the various activities and team building events. Saturday evening promises to be great fun and I’m intrigued to see what a ‘Vegas’ themed evening delivers! Sunday morning provides everybody with the opportunity to blow away the cobwebs.

If you have questions or problems over the course over the weekend, let me or Tanya know, and we’ll do what we can.
Thank you for coming. I hope you all have a fantastic weekend.
