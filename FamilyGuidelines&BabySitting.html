Family guidelines & Baby sitting

Pre-booked babysitting service 7pm-1am

Responsibilities
• Babysitters must have been pre-booked prior to the weekend with Tanya Gabbitas
• The babysitting manager will be at Reception
• Please meet the babysitting manager from 6:30pm on Saturday evening to review the completed booking form and ensure they have your exact requirements
• All children must be in pyjamas and in bed before the babysitters take responsibility
• Babysitters will be outside the bedrooms. Please ensure that you prop the door open and leave a key with them, as each babysitter will be covering more than one room
• Parents must always be contactable - babysitters will call the parents if their child wakes up and does not settle
• Parents must return to the room promptly (1am) - babysitters cannot finish duty until you return
• Once the babysitter has finished, we strongly recommend you do not leave your children unattended in the room. Unattended children are your responsibility

Movie and games rooms
• The children’s movie and games rooms will be supervised by the babysitters
• Young children must be signed in and out of these rooms
• The games room has an open-door policy (children can come in/out)
• It is the parent’s responsibility to ensure that their child is happy to stay in these rooms and they are not wandering round the hotel unaccompanied
• Snacks/cordial and water will be available throughout
