Check-in & check-out

A few things to enjoy your weekend...

Reception
Saturday Check-in 3pm – bags to be left at reception and collected later
Gym and pool facilities – if you want to use the gym during your ask reception for the code
Sunday Check-out 11am – bags can be left at reception and you can still use all facilities until your departure
Wristbands
When you check-in each guest will be given a wristband for free drinks at the bar so please keep them with you during your stay. If you have children, please add your contact details to their wristbands.
Beverages
The bar will be limited to a selection of wines, beers and spirits & soft drinks any other option drinks need to be settled directly with the hotel
