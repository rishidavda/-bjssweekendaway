A few things to enjoy your weekend..

Check-in & check-out

Check-in on Saturday is at 3 pm – bags can be left at reception and collected later.

Check-out on Sunday is at 11 am – bags can be left at reception, leisure facilities will be available until departure.

Wristbands

A wristband will be issued to each guest on arrival. It will also be your free drinks pass at the bar, so please keep it on during your stay.

If you have children, please add your contact details to their wristbands.

Leisure facilities

Gym – if you want to use the gym, please ask reception for the code.

Pool, sauna and steam room - you can use them until your depature.

Showers - you can use them until depature.

Beverages

The BJSS inclusive bar will be limited to a selection of wines, beers, spirits & soft drinks. Any other drinks will need to be settled direcrly with the hotel at guests' own expense.
